A themed resource pack that replaces all food textures with custom KasaiSora-themed designs. Perfect for fans of KasaiSora or anyone looking for unique food textures.

## Features

- **Themed Textures**: Custom KasaiSora-themed food designs
- **16x Resolution**: Clean, detailed textures
- **Complete Coverage**: All food items replaced
- **Regular Updates**: Continuously maintained
- **Vanilla Compatible**: Works with vanilla Minecraft
