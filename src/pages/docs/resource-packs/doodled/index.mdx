Doodled is a unique 64x resolution resource pack featuring hand-drawn textures. Every texture has been carefully hand-drawn to give your Minecraft world a distinctive, artistic look.

## Features

- **Hand Drawn**: Every texture is hand-drawn
- **64x Resolution**: High-quality detailed textures
- **Simplistic Style**: Clean, artistic aesthetic
- **Complete Coverage**: All blocks and items redrawn
- **Regular Updates**: Continuously expanded

## Installation

1. Download Doodled from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/resource-pack/doodled)
2. Open Minecraft and go to Options â†’ Resource Packs
3. Click "Open Pack Folder"
4. Copy the downloaded ZIP file into the folder
5. Select Doodled in the resource packs menu
6. Click "Done" and enjoy the hand-drawn aesthetic!

## Style

Doodled features a unique hand-drawn art style that gives Minecraft a completely different feel. Every texture has been carefully crafted by hand to create a cohesive, artistic look.

## Requirements

- Minecraft 1.20.1 or later
- No mods required (though OptiFine recommended for best experience)

## Tips

- Works best with OptiFine for additional customization
- Higher resolution may impact performance on low-end systems
- Perfect for creative builds and artistic projects
- Can be combined with shaders for stunning visuals
